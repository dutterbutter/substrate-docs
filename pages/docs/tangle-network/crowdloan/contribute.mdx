# Contribute to Crowdloan

The best way to contribute to the Tangle Network crowdloan is through the official Tangle Network crowdloan
page using the Polkadot browser extension.

### Check prerequisites

- I have installed the Polkadot browser extension.
- I have KSM in my Polkadot.js account.
- My KSM has been 'unbonded'. Click [here](https://wiki.polkadot.network/docs/maintain-guides-how-to-unbond) for instructions on unbonding if you're staking KSM in Polkadot.js, or find the unstake/unbond option in your mobile wallet.

## Contribute to the crowdloan

1. Navigate to Tangle Network official crowdloan page.
2. Connect the Polkadot extension wallet, you shall see your KSM balance.
3. To participate in the crowdloan, you must read and accept the terms and conditions. You are required to sign with the account used to participate in the crowdloan, but it will not cost you anything.
4. Enter the KSM contribution amount. For every KSM contributed, you will receive at least 25 TNT tokens.
5. Click the 'Submit Contribution' button to submit your contribution to the Kusama Relay Chain. This may take a few seconds while Kusama finalizes your transaction.

You can contribute multiple times with the same account. Share your great contribution on social media!
